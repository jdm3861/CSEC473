About Our Facility

Riverside Nuclear Power Plant is a state-of-the-art facility utilizing advanced pressurized water reactor technology. Our plant generates clean, reliable electricity for over 800,000 homes across the tri-state region.


With four decades of safe operation, we maintain the highest standards of nuclear safety and environmental stewardship while providing carbon-free baseload power to support our community's energy needs.
